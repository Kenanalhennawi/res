Quick:

Introduction

RezGui extends FZ schedules and PNR list display for Senior Commercial Management. Use cryptic entries to retrieve PNRs and lists. Supports GDS FZ Prime, Holiday PNRs, Codeshare, OTA, and Direct channel bookings.

PNR Display – Retrieve

*<PNR> – Retrieve PNR and display complete itinerary with applicable follow-up entries. Works for GDS FZ Prime and Holiday PNRs.

PNR Header shows: Confirmation Number, Channel. Time Limit shown only if PNR has balance due.

L Follow-up – PNR Selection from List

Example: Enter LBAFZ001J10JAN (10 names). Enter L*4 → PNR on line 4 displays. Enter L*N → next PNR (line 5). Enter L** → back to name list.

EntryDescription
L*<serial no>Retrieve PNR at that line (e.g. L*4). Triggers internal *<pnr>.
L*N or L*nDisplay next PNR in list (from L*<serial> or L*N).
L**Back to name list. List shows all pax per last L criteria.

Errors: Invalid serial → "INVALID SEQUENCE NUMBER". Unsupported entry → generic error.
